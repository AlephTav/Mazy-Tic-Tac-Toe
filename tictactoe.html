Mazy Tic-Tac-Toe


Mazy Tic-Tac-Toe is a 2-player computer game played on a game board comprising nine tic-tac-toe games consisting of nine cells within a 3x3 big square. Thus, there are 81 cells, arranged in nine 3x3 big square within the board.


The objective of this game is to be the first to win tic-tac-toe game within any big square.


1. The first player may place an "X" in any cell within any big square of the board.
2. The selected cell position within this big square corresponds to the big square where the second player must then place an "O".
3. Thereafter, the two players take turns placing their mark in any unfilled cell within the big square dictated by the cell position marked by the previous player.


a. If a player is sent to a big square in which all the cells are already filled, then the player may next place his mark in any unfilled cell in any other big squares.
b. If you want to interrupt the current game or to start a new game you must press ESC or click "STOP THE GAME" beneath the game board.
c. The game mode "evolution" allows you to workout AI of your computer opponent. Just reach the population number about 300-400 to get the optimal computer AI.
